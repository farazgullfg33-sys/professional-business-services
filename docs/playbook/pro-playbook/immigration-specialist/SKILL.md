# Immigration Specialist

## Role
Senior Immigration Consultant in a UAE PRO office.

## Core Function
Golden visa, green visa, freelancer visa, investor visa, retirement visa, specialized residency programs, nomination-heavy cases, and high-value immigration advisory.

## Golden Visa (10-Year) Categories
1. **Investors**: AED 2M+ in public investment (deposit, company, fund, property)
2. **Entrepreneurs**: Owner/partner of startup with AED 500K+ annual revenue, or approved incubator
3. **Exceptional Talents**: Doctors, scientists, inventors, artists, athletes (nomination required)
4. **Outstanding Students**: Top UAE secondary school or university (GPA 3.8+)
5. **Humanitarian Pioneers**: Recognized humanitarian workers
6. **Frontline Heroes**: Healthcare workers during crises

## Green Visa (5-Year)
- **Freelancers/self-employed**: Bachelor's degree + AED 360K annual income
- **Skilled employees**: Valid employment contract, salary AED 15K+, bachelor's degree
- **Investors/partners**: Approved investment + partner approval

## Freelancer Visa
- GoFreelance (TECOM): Dubai Media City, Dubai Internet City, Dubai Knowledge Park
- Twofour54 (Abu Dhabi): Media freelancers
- RAKEZ, Ajman Freezone options
- Required: Portfolio, bank statements (AED 5K-10K+ monthly), NOC from current sponsor if employed

## Investor Visa
- 3-year investor visa for company partners
- Partner visa: based on trade license investment amount
- Property investor visa: AED 750K+ property value (2-year)

## Retirement Visa (5-Year)
- Age 55+
- Criteria: AED 1M+ savings OR AED 15K+ monthly income OR AED 750K+ property OR combination

## Step-by-Step (Golden Visa — Investor)
1. Verify investment amount and category eligibility
2. Collect: passport, photos, bank statements, investment proof, health insurance
3. Nomination (if talent category): prepare portfolio, recommendation letters
4. Submit via ICP Golden Visa portal
5. Medical fitness test
6. Emirates ID + visa stamping
7. Processing: 30-90 days

## Fee Structure (AED)
- Golden Visa: 2,800-4,000 (government fees) + PRO service 5,000-15,000
- Green Visa: 2,500-3,500 + PRO service 3,000-8,000
- Freelancer Visa: 7,500-15,000 (all-in) + PRO service 3,000-5,000

## Common Issues
- Minimum investment not met: verify exact category requirements
- Nomination required but unavailable: check alternative categories
- Quota reached: some categories have annual caps
- Previous visa ban: address ban first through ICP appeal
- Documentation translation and attestation: factor additional 2-4 weeks

## Dependencies
- pro-visa-processor (standard visa steps), pro-document-attester (degree/certificate attestation), pro-compliance-officer (legal review)
