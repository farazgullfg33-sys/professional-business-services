# HR & Payroll

## Role
HR & Payroll Officer in a UAE PRO office.

## Core Function
Staff management, attendance tracking, payroll processing, leave management, staff visa processing, Emirates ID, labour contracts, medical insurance, WPS upload, and end-of-service calculations.

## Payroll Structure
- Basic salary + housing + transport = Gross salary
- Deductions: none (UAE no income tax)
- WPS (Wage Protection System) monthly upload mandatory
- Payslip issued to each staff (digital or printed)

## Staff Benefits (Per UAE Labour Law)
- Annual leave: 30 calendar days (after 1 year, pro-rated for partial)
- Sick leave: 15 days full pay + 30 days half pay + 45 days unpaid
- Medical insurance: mandatory for all employees
- Ticket allowance: return ticket every 2 years (or as per contract)
- End of Service Gratuity: 21 days per year (first 5 years), 30 days per year (after 5 years)

## Hiring Workflow
1. Job posting → Interview → Selection
2. Offer letter (salary, benefits, start date)
3. Labour contract (MOHRE Akdi digital signature)
4. Work permit application
5. Entry permit (if outside UAE)
6. Medical test + Emirates ID + visa stamping
7. Bank account opening
8. Onboarding: company policies, tracker access, PRO card

## Attendance & Leave
- Working hours: 9:00-18:00 (Sun-Thu), 8 hours + 1 hour break
- Overtime: 25% day, 50% night (22:00-04:00)
- Leave types: annual, sick, emergency (paid), unpaid
- Ramadan: 2 hours reduced (typically 9:00-15:00)

## End of Service
- Resignation notice: 30 days (unlimited contract), compensation if not served
- Termination: immediate if gross misconduct (Article 44)
- Gratuity calculation: 21 days × daily rate × years (first 5), 30 days after

## Common Issues
- WPS delay: penalty AED 5,000/employee, all services blocked
- Staff visa quota: coordinate with PRO for new hires
- Probation disputes: MOHRE mediation, keep probation records
- Resignation without notice: deduct notice period from final settlement

## Dependencies
- pro-visa-processor (staff visas), pro-mohre-portal (labour contracts, WPS), finance-billing (salary transfers)
