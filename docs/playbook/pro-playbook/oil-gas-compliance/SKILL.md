# Oil & Gas Compliance

## Role
Oil & Gas Compliance Specialist in a UAE PRO office.

## Core Function
ICV certification, ISO certification (9001, 14001, 45001), contractor classification, ADNOC registration, supplier pre-qualification, HSE documentation, and tender-readiness.

## Key Services

### ICV Certification (In-Country Value)
- Portal: https://icv.gov.ae
- Score based on: Goods manufactured in UAE, Third-party spend in UAE, UAE investment, Emiratisation %, Expat contributions
- Required for: ADNOC and major NOC tenders
- Certification: Annual, audited financial statements required
- Minimum target: 5%+ for new suppliers

### ISO Certification Path
1. Gap analysis (assess current state)
2. Documentation (policies, procedures, work instructions)
3. Implementation (train staff, run processes)
4. Internal audit (test the system)
5. Certification audit (external auditor)
6. Surveillance audits (annual)
- **ISO 9001**: Quality Management
- **ISO 14001**: Environmental Management
- **ISO 45001**: Occupational Health & Safety
- Timeline: 3-6 months per certification

### ADNOC Registration
- Portal: ADNOC Supplier Registration Portal
- Steps: Company profile → Commercial → HSE prequal → Technical → Financial → Approval
- Documents: Trade license, MOA, Chamber cert, ISO certs, ICV cert, HSE policy, financial statements, bank details

### Contractor Classification
- National Qualification Center (NQC) classification
- Categories based on: Financial capability, technical capability, past projects, manpower
- Required for government/semi-government tenders

## Fee Structure (AED)
- ICV certification: 5,000-15,000
- ISO 9001: 15,000-30,000
- ISO 14001: 15,000-25,000
- ISO 45001: 15,000-25,000
- ADNOC registration: 5,000-10,000
- NQC classification: 5,000-15,000

## Common Issues
- Low ICV score: improve Emiratisation %, local procurement
- ISO audit failed: close non-conformities within 30 days, re-audit
- ADNOC portal rejection: check document completeness, format requirements
- Missing HSE documentation: develop HSE manual, record incidents

## Dependencies
- pro-compliance-officer (regulatory compliance), pro-company-former (new company setup for O&G), finance-billing (cost proposals)
