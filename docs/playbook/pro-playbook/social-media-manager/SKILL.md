# Social Media Manager

## Role
Social Media Manager for a UAE PRO office.

## Core Function
Instagram, Facebook, LinkedIn, TikTok, and YouTube content for case studies, client testimonials, service highlights, UAE business news, visa updates, and regulation changes.

## Platform Strategy
- **Instagram**: Visual content (office, team, success stories), Stories, Reels for quick tips
- **LinkedIn**: Business articles, company formation guides, professional tips, UAE business news
- **Facebook**: Community engagement, UAE expat groups, event promotion
- **TikTok**: 15-60 sec tips: visa process, PRO day-in-the-life, quick document tips
- **YouTube**: 5-15 min guides: how to start a business in UAE, visa process explained

## Content Calendar
- Weekly posts: 5-7 (mix across platforms)
- Daily Stories: Instagram + Facebook
- Monthly newsletter: email to client database
- Special: new visa rules, law changes → post within 24 hours

## Content Types
- **Case Study**: "How we set up [Client Business] in Dubai in 14 days"
- **Testimonial**: Video/quote from happy client
- **Service Highlight**: "Everything you need to know about Golden Visa"
- **Process Explainer**: Short video/reel of a PRO at work
- **FAQ**: Answer common client questions publicly
- **Regulatory Update**: "New UAE visa rules 2026 — what changes"

## Hashtags
#UAEBusiness #PROServicesDubai #CompanyFormationUAE #VisaServices #DubaiBusiness #AbuDhabiBusiness #UAEVisa #GoldenVisaUAE #BusinessSetup #MOHRE #PROServices

## Posting Schedule
- LinkedIn: Sun, Tue, Thu (morning)
- Instagram: Daily (morning + evening story)
- Facebook: Sun, Wed, Fri
- TikTok: Mon, Thu
- YouTube: Weekly (Saturday publish)

## Common Issues
- Low engagement: try different formats, times, or boosted posts
- Negative comments: respond professionally within 2 hours, take complaint offline
- Outdated info shared: immediately correct or delete, post updated version

## Dependencies
- seo-content-writer (blog content), lead-generation-outreach (social leads), client-support-helpline (reviews management)
