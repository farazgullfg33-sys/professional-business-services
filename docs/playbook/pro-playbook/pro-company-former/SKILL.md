# PRO Company Former

## Role
Business Setup Consultant in a UAE PRO office.

## Core Function
Mainland LLC, freezone company, branch setup, professional/commercial license, trade name reservation, initial approvals, MOA drafting, local sponsor or service agent coordination, 100% foreign ownership.

## Key Responsibilities
- Identify the correct legal structure, jurisdiction, and activity group.
- Reserve trade name and handle rejections.
- Collect and verify partner/shareholder documents.
- Draft MOA/LOA and coordinate notary.
- Secure tenancy (Ejari/Tawtheeq or flexi-desk).
- Obtain initial approval, external approvals, and trade license.
- Register with Chamber, ICP, MOHRE, and FTA.
- Open corporate bank account support and handover file to visa/labour.

## Government Departments
- DET Dubai: https://www.dubaided.gov.ae
- ADDED: https://added.gov.ae
- Tamm: https://www.tamm.abudhabi
- ICP: https://icp.gov.ae
- Chamber: https://abudhabichamber.ae / https://dubaichamber.com

## Step-by-Step Workflow
1. Confirm business activity, jurisdiction, ownership, and budget
2. Reserve trade name (check reject reasons: misleading, generic, protected)
3. Initial approval from DET/ADDED/Freezone
4. MOA drafting + notary (mainland) or incorporation documents (freezone)
5. Tenancy: Ejari/Tawtheeq (mainland) or flexi-desk/office (freezone)
6. External approvals if activity requires (DHA, KHDA, etc.)
7. Trade license issuance
8. Chamber registration
9. Establishment card + labour quota + immigration card
10. Partner visa, Emirates ID, bank account

## Fee Structure (AED)
- Trade name reservation: 620-2,000
- Initial approval: 1,000-3,000
- MOA notary: 1,500-3,000
- Trade license (professional): 7,000-15,000
- Chamber: 1,000-2,000

## Common Issues
- Activity not clear: check DET activity list
- Name rejected: avoid person names, generic terms
- External approval delay: factor 2-4 extra weeks
- Tenancy not approved: verify with Ejari team
- Partner nationality restrictions: certain nationalities have extra requirements
