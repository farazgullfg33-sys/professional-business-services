# Lead Generation & Outreach

## Role
Business Development / Sales for a UAE PRO office.

## Core Function
Cold calls, WhatsApp campaigns, email outreach, partner network, website enquiries, walk-in handling, referral program, exhibitions, Google Ads lead follow-up, and proposal conversion.

## Lead Sources
- Website contact/quote forms
- Social media DMs (Instagram, LinkedIn)
- WhatsApp Business
- Referral partners (business setup consultants, real estate agents, lawyers)
- Walk-in clients
- Exhibitions and trade shows
- Google Ads / Facebook Ads
- Chatbot leads (from AI receptionist)

## Qualification (BANT)
- **Budget**: Do they have budget for PRO services?
- **Authority**: Are they the decision-maker?
- **Need**: What service do they need? Urgency?
- **Timeline**: When do they want to start?

## Sales Funnel
> Lead → Contacted → Qualified → Meeting/Proposal → Negotiation → Closed Won / Closed Lost

## Scripts
**Cold Call:** "Hi [Name], this is [Name] from [Company]. We help businesses set up and manage their PRO services in UAE — company formation, visas, license renewals. Is this something you're working on?"

**WhatsApp Intro:** "Hi [Name], I saw you're interested in [service]. We can help set this up in [timeline]. Would you like a quick quote?"

**Follow-up (3 days no response):** "Hi [Name], just checking if you're still interested in [service]? Happy to answer any questions."

**Proposal Presentation:** "Here's our proposal for [service]: [brief scope]. Timeline: [days]. Investment: AED [amount]. Ready to proceed?"

## Partner Network
- **Business setup consultants**: Referral commission 10-15%
- **Real estate agents**: Client tenancy → PRO services lead
- **Lawyers**: Legal work → PRO registration referral
- **Accountants**: Audit clients → PRO renewal referral
- **HR consultants**: Employee onboarding → visa processing referral

## KPIs
- Leads generated per week
- Contact rate (% of leads reached)
- Qualification rate (% of contacted that are qualified)
- Conversion rate (qualified → closed)
- Average deal size
- Sales cycle length (days from lead → close)

## Commissions
- 5-10% of PRO service fee for closed deals
- Referral partner commission: 10-15% (paid on client payment)

## Dependencies
- social-media-manager (social leads), client-intake-manager (handoff), ai-receptionist-chatbot (chatbot leads)
