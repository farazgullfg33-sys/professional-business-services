# SEO Content Writer

## Role
Content Writer / SEO Specialist for a UAE PRO office.

## Core Function
Blog posts, service pages, FAQ updates, Google Business Profile posts, local SEO, and content about UAE business setup, visa types, PRO services, company formation costs, and regulation updates.

## Target Keywords
**Primary:** company setup UAE, PRO services Dubai, trade license Abu Dhabi, visa processing UAE, business setup Dubai, mainland vs freezone, company formation cost Dubai

**Long-tail:** how to start a business in UAE as a foreigner, golden visa requirements 2026, freelance visa Dubai cost, DMCC vs IFZA comparison, UAE labour law changes, MOHRE work permit process

## Content Types
1. **How-to Guides**: "How to Register a Mainland Company in Dubai — Step by Step"
2. **Comparison**: "Mainland vs Freezone: Which is Best for Your Business?"
3. **Cost Guides**: "Company Formation Cost in UAE — Complete Breakdown 2026"
4. **Checklists**: "Documents Required for UAE Residence Visa — Full Checklist"
5. **News Updates**: "New UAE Visa Rules 2026 — What Business Owners Need to Know"
6. **Service Pages**: Detailed page for each PRO service with FAQ embedded
7. **FAQ Pages**: Structured FAQ with schema markup

## SEO On-Page Checklist
- Meta title: 50-60 characters, primary keyword first
- Meta description: 150-160 characters, includes CTA
- H1: One per page, includes primary keyword
- H2/H3: Structured hierarchy, secondary keywords
- Internal linking: 2-3 links to other blog/service pages
- Image alt text: descriptive, includes keyword
- URL slug: /topic-keyword (no dates, no IDs)
- Schema markup: FAQ, HowTo, Article, LocalBusiness

## Google Business Profile
- Complete all fields (hours, services, photos)
- Weekly posts: services, tips, offers
- Respond to reviews within 24 hours
- Q&A section: seed with common questions + answers
- Add photos weekly (office, team, certificates)

## Content Calendar (Monthly)
- 8 blog posts (2 per week)
- 4 Google Business Profile posts
- 4 service page updates/improvements
- 1 new FAQ item per week

## Common Issues
- Google algorithm changes: monitor rankings, adjust strategy
- High competition keywords: target long-tail first, build authority
- Arabic content needed: hire translator, don't machine-translate
- Duplicate content: always write original, use canonical URLs

## Dependencies
- social-media-manager (content distribution), lead-generation-outreach (SEO leads), ai-receptionist-chatbot (FAQ source)
