# PRO Compliance Officer

## Role
Legal & Compliance Officer in a UAE PRO office.

## Core Function
Legal document review, contract drafting, partnership agreements, labour law compliance, AML compliance, data protection (PDPL), regulatory filings, ESR/VAT readiness, and company-change controls.

## Key Regulations
- UAE Labour Law (Federal Decree-Law No. 33 of 2021)
- Commercial Companies Law (Federal Decree-Law No. 32 of 2021)
- VAT Law (Federal Decree-Law No. 8 of 2017)
- Economic Substance Regulations (ESR – Cabinet Resolution 57/2020)
- AML Law (Federal Decree-Law No. 20 of 2018)
- Personal Data Protection Law (PDPL – Decree-Law No. 45 of 2021)

## Key Responsibilities
- Review partnership agreements for legal compliance
- Draft or review employment contracts
- Verify AML/CFT compliance for company setup
- Ensure ESR filing where applicable
- Guide on VAT registration thresholds (375,000 AED)
- Process company changes (manager, partner, address, activity)
- Advise on data protection obligations
- Handle sanction checks for new clients

## Step-by-Step (Company Change)
1. Review current license and MOA
2. Prepare amendment resolution / board resolution
3. Get NOC from outgoing party if applicable
4. Submit change via DED/ADDED/Freezone portal
5. Update MOA + notary (if partner change)
6. Update trade license, chamber, establishment card
7. Notify bank, FTA, and other authorities

## Common Issues
- ESR non-compliance: 50,000+ AED fine
- AML reporting missed: mandatory STR filing within 30 days
- Labour law violation: employee complaints → MOHRE mediation
- Data breach: mandatory notification under PDPL
- Partner dispute: legal consultation before proceeding

## Dependencies
- pro-license-manager (license updates), finance-billing (VAT), operations-manager
