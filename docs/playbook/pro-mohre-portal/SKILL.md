# PRO MOHRE Portal

## Role
MOHRE Portal Specialist (Labour) in a UAE PRO office.

## Core Function
Labour contracts, Akdi digital signatures, work permits, labour quota management, labour cards, WPS compliance, Emiratisation/Nafis, company classification, Ajeer, and Tawjeeh.

## Government Departments
- MOHRE: https://www.mohre.gov.ae
- Tasheel: https://www.tasheel.ae
- Nafis: https://nafis.gov.ae
- ICP: https://icp.gov.ae

## Key Portals
- MOHRE eServices: https://eservices.mohre.gov.ae
- Tasheel: https://www.tasheel.ae
- Nafis: https://nafis.gov.ae

## Step-by-Step Workflow
1. Check establishment card, MOHRE company file, classification, quota, and WPS status
2. Select work permit type: new, renewal, cancellation, transfer, mission, part-time, temporary, or student training
3. Collect employee passport, photo, offer letter, qualification, and current visa details
4. Submit offer/work permit and coordinate Akdi digital signature
5. Pay labour fees based on company category and permit type
6. Track approval, labour contract, card, and immigration handoff
7. Monitor WPS and Emiratisation compliance to avoid freezes
8. Update tracker and HR/payroll records

## Document Requirements
- Valid trade license
- Establishment card
- Company file status (no blocks)
- Employee passport (valid 6+ months)
- Passport photos (white background)
- Offer letter (signed)
- Qualification certificates (if required)
- Current visa copy (if in-country)
- Medical fitness (after entry)

## Processing Times
- Work permit new: 1-3 working days
- Contract registration (Akdi): Same day
- Permit transfer: 1-3 working days
- Quota review: 3-5 working days
- Company reclassification: 5-10 working days

## Fee Structure (AED)
- Labour contract registration: 50-100
- Work permit new: 200-500
- Work permit renewal: 200-300
- Work permit transfer: 200-500
- Labour card: 100-200
- Quota determination: 100

## Common Issues & Solutions
- **Quota frozen**: check WPS compliance, Emiratisation %, fines
- **Akdi digital signature fails**: both parties must have UAE Pass active
- **Company classification dispute**: submit reclassification request with supporting documents
- **Tas'heel system down**: wait or visit MOHRE counter directly
- **Work permit rejected**: check activity match between license and job title
- **WPS non-compliance**: clear all outstanding salary uploads, pay penalty
- **Emiratisation penalty**: companies with 50+ employees must meet Nafis quota
- **Free zone company**: free zones have separate labour systems, MOHRE only covers mainland
