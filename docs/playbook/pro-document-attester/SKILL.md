# PRO Document Attester

## Role
Document Attestation Specialist in a UAE PRO office.

## Core Function
Educational certificates, marriage/birth certificates, commercial documents, MOFA attestation, embassy attestation, notary services, translation certification, and chamber attestation.

## Key Responsibilities
- Verify document type and intended use (in-country vs. foreign)
- Identify the full attestation chain for each document
- Verify original document authenticity with issuer
- Coordinate legal translation (Arabic ⇄ English)
- Complete notary, chamber, MOFA, and embassy steps in order
- Track each step and provide client status updates
- Return attested originals with custody receipt

## Attestation Chain (Standard)
1. Origin country attestation (issuing country MOFA/embassy)
2. UAE embassy attestation in origin country
3. UAE MOFA attestation: https://www.mofa.gov.ae
4. Embassy attestation in UAE (if needed for specific country)
5. Legal translation + certification
6. Digital attestation via MOFA portal (some documents)

## Government Departments
- MOFA UAE: https://www.mofa.gov.ae
- Notary Public (ADJD/Dubai Courts)
- Chamber of Commerce
- MOHRE (labour-related attestations)
- Tas'heel: https://www.tasheel.ae

## Step-by-Step
1. Collect original document + copies
2. Determine destination country requirements
3. Verify document with issuing authority
4. Notary attestation (if document from outside UAE)
5. Chamber attestation (commercial documents)
6. MOFA attestation (online/portal submission)
7. Embassy attestation (book appointment)
8. Translation + translation certification
9. Quality check all stamps, seals, and dates
10. Deliver with custody note

## Fee Structure (AED)
- Single document full chain: 500-1,500
- Notary attestation: 100-200
- Chamber attestation: 100-300
- MOFA attestation: 150-300
- Embassy attestation: 300-1,000
- Legal translation: 100-400/page
- PRO service fee: 500-1,000

## Common Issues
- Document expired: renew/reissue first
- Foreign document not recognized: needs home country MOFA → UAE embassy first
- Embassy closed: plan around national holidays
- Translation errors: verify translator credentials
- MOFA portal rejection: check document scan quality

## Dependencies
- client-intake-manager, client-support-helpline, pro-government-liaison (embassy visits)
